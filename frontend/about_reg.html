About Mark Your Territory

Have you ever wondered why dogs get so much joy out of peeing everywhere they go? It’s because they’re marking their territory! And now you have the chance to do the same.

Welcome to Mark Your Territory—the ultimate way to claim your space in the world, one bathroom break at a time! Our game lets you track every spot you've peed and proudly displays your “territories” on a global map for all to see. Whether you’re traveling, exploring, or just taking care of business, you can mark your scent and leave your mark—because why just go when you can *go global*? Join the fun and let’s see who can conquer the world, one restroom at a time!
