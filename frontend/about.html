About Mark Your Territory

Welcome to Mark Your Territory—the ultimate way to claim your space in the world, one bathroom break at a time! Our app lets you track every spot you've peed and proudly displays your “territories” on a global map for all to see. Whether you’re traveling, exploring, or just taking care of business, you can mark your scent and leave your mark—because why just go when you can *go global*? Join the fun and let’s see who can conquer the world, one restroom at a time!
