Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mark Your Territory?

Mark Your Territory is an app that allows users to log and track where they have peed with the hopes of taking over the world

How do I log an entry?

Go to the home page and respond to the prompt underneath "Create New Entry:". Your location will be automatically marked for you when you hit enter.

What is the diffence between the "Your Territory" map and the Global map?

The "Your Territory" map shows all of your personal territory (all of your entries), while the Global map shows entries from people all over the world.

What should I do if things are not loading properly?

Log out and log back in and everything should work as expected. If the issue persists, contact us.

Why aren't all of my marks showing on the map?

Only the most recent mark in each territory appears on the map.
